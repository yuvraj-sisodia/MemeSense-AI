MemeSense AI

Terms & Privacy

1. Project Purpose

MemeSense AI is an educational Minor Project developed by Data Science students. It demonstrates the application of Natural Language Processing (VADER Sentiment Analysis), Optical Character Recognition (OCR), and Context-Aware API Routing. It is not a commercial product.

2. Privacy & Data Handling (Important)

We operate on a Privacy-First Architecture. Here is exactly how your data is handled:

  • Image Processing: All OCR (Text Extraction) happens Client-Side in your browser using Tesseract.js. Your images are processed in your device's RAM and are never uploaded to our servers.
  • Text Analysis: Only the extracted text (strings) is sent to our backend for Sentiment Analysis. This text is processed in real-time and is not stored in any database.
  • No Cookies: We do not use cookies or tracking pixels.

3. Content Sources

Meme search results are fetched dynamically from the public Reddit API via meme-api.com. We do not host or own any meme images. Content is subject to the availability and moderation of the respective Subreddits (e.g., r/ProgrammerHumor, r/wholesomememes).

4. Limitation of Liability

This tool is for demonstration purposes only. The sentiment analysis AI (VADER) is probabilistic and may occasionally misinterpret sarcasm or complex context. We are not responsible for the content of memes fetched from third-party APIs.
